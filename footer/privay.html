Privacy Policy

Imperial Heights Modern School – Commitment to Excellence, Integrity, and Trust

At Imperial Heights Modern School, we uphold the highest standards of discretion, responsibility, and care when it comes to your personal information. As a premier institution built on values of excellence, integrity, and trust, we are committed to safeguarding the privacy of our students, parents, and community members. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information in line with global best practices and applicable data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide an exceptional educational experience, ensure a secure environment, and comply with legal requirements. This may include:

  • Parent and student names, addresses, contact numbers, and demographic details
  • Admission and enrollment records, including documents provided during the application process
  • Academic performance, assessments, attendance, and progress reports
  • Medical and health-related information (where required for student safety and welfare)
  • Extracurricular participation, achievements, and interests
  • Website usage data (cookies, analytics, inquiry forms, communication logs)
  • Photographs and video recordings for school records, ID cards, and authorized publications

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for legitimate educational and administrative purposes, including:

  • Maintaining accurate student records and tracking academic progress
  • Ensuring safety, security, and well-being on campus
  • Facilitating communication between the school and parents
  • Organizing school events, activities, and cultural or academic programs
  • Complying with regulatory and legal obligations
  • Improving our website, admissions process, and digital services
  • Promoting student achievements (with prior consent) in newsletters, website, or brochures

3. Information Security

We adopt world-class security protocols to ensure your data is safeguarded against unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. Access to personal information is restricted to authorized school personnel only, and sensitive data is protected using encryption, secure servers, and password controls. Regular audits and monitoring are conducted to uphold the highest standards of confidentiality.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data may only be shared with:

  • Trusted service providers (e.g., IT support, transport, healthcare, or educational platforms) for school-related purposes
  • Regulatory authorities when legally required
  • Third parties for authorized events, examinations, or competitions — always with safeguards in place

All external partners are bound by confidentiality agreements to ensure data protection.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections if your data is inaccurate or incomplete
  • Request deletion of your personal information where applicable
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential uses (e.g., publicity photographs)
  • Raise concerns regarding misuse or mishandling of your data

6. Cookies & Digital Privacy

Our website uses cookies and tracking technologies to improve user experience and optimize performance. Cookies do not identify individuals but help us analyze browsing behavior. You may disable cookies through your browser, though some features may not function optimally.

7. Policy Updates

Imperial Heights Modern School reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect evolving standards, technology, or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page, and significant changes will be communicated to parents and guardians through official school channels.
