Early Childhood Program

Nursery – KG | Ages 3-5 Years

Program Overview

Our Early Childhood Program provides a nurturing and inspiring foundation for young learners aged 3–5 years. Designed to spark imagination, creativity, and confidence, our curriculum blends structured exploration with the freedom of play in a safe, vibrant, and internationally enriched environment.

Program Highlights

  • Play-based learning with a global perspective
  • Creative arts, music, and movement enrichment
  • Language development through storytelling
  • Social-emotional growth in a multicultural setting
  • State-of-the-art classrooms & outdoor play areas

Innovative Syllabus

  • Global Cultures Exploration: Celebrating festivals & traditions worldwide
  • STEM for Early Years: Simple science, robotics & coding blocks
  • Environmental Awareness: Gardening, recycling & sustainability
  • Mindfulness & Emotional Intelligence: Breathing, gratitude & empathy
  • Multilingual Exposure: Introduction to international languages
  • Performing Arts: Early music theory, dance & expression
