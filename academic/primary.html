Primary Education

Grades 1–5 | Ages 6–10 Years

Overview

Our Primary Education program builds a strong foundation in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking. With hands-on learning experiences, students develop confidence and a lifelong love for knowledge.

Program Highlights

  • Strong Core Subjects: Math, Science, Languages
  • Early STEM & Technology Integration
  • Language Development & Communication Skills
  • Character Education & Global Citizenship

Innovative Syllabus

  • Global Studies & Cultural Awareness
  • Performing Arts: Music, Dance & Drama
  • Creative Visual Arts & Design
  • Environmental Science & Sustainability Projects
  • Introduction to Coding & Educational Games
  • Social Skills, Teamwork & Leadership
