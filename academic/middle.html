Middle School

Grades 6–8 | Ages 11–13 Years

Overview

Our Middle School program prepares students for advanced studies by fostering analytical and problem-solving skills. A balanced curriculum ensures academic growth along with leadership opportunities.

Program Highlights

  • Advanced Mathematics & Logical Reasoning
  • Hands-on Science Laboratories
  • Research & Independent Projects
  • Leadership & Community Engagement

Innovative Syllabus

  • Introduction to Coding & Robotics
  • Environmental Studies & Global Challenges
  • Critical Thinking & Problem Solving Modules
  • Visual Arts, Music & Drama Integration
  • Wellness, Mindfulness & Life Skills
  • STEM Innovation Labs & Experiments
